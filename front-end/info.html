Alcohol Restrictions

The lock will be activated when you have more then 0.28mg/l alcohol in your blood. This is circa 0.7 promille. From that point you are not allowed to drive for about 3 hours. When you have more then 0.40mg/l alcohol in your blood you are restricted for 6h. This is about 0.9 promille.

Belgian Fines For Drunk Driving.

  • 0.28mg/l - 0.40mg/l: 200€ - 4.000€
  • >0.40mg/l : 1.600€ - 16.000€
  • Unable or refusing to take an alcohol test: 1.600€ - 16.000€
